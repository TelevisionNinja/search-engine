David Beckham Brings Son Brooklyn to Victoria Beckham's New York Fashion Week Show—See the Photo!

by Corinne Heller Sun., Sep. 7, 2014 2:19 PM PDT

Loading...
AP Photo/Richard Drew

Victoria Beckham got a sweet show of support at her recent New York Fashion Week runway show from two of her favorite guys!

Husband and retired soccer star David Beckham and son Brooklyn Beckham, 15, were spotted sitting front row at the Spice Girls singer and fashion designer's Spring / Summer 2015 show on Sunday.

Sitting next to them was none other than Vogue Editor in Chief Anna Wintour.

David looked suave in a black suit and white open-collar, button-down shirt, while his and Victoria's son sported a black jacket with the sleeves rolled up and a black T-shirt, as well as a gold necklace.

News: Brooklyn Beckham breaks up with girlfriend to spend more time with Chloë Grace Moretz

He and his siblings have occasionally joined their parents at major events. He and brothers, Romeo, 12, and Cruz, 9, and sister Harper, 3, sat with David to watch their mother's Fall 2014 show in February. The boys also accompanied their dad at the 2014 FIFA World Cup.

Earlier this year, Brooklyn made his modeling debut in Man About Town Magazine.
