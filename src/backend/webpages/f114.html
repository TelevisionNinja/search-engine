Important information about your djindexes.com registration

As you may know, Dow Jones Indexes recently merged with S&P Indices to form S&P Dow Jones Indices. Over the coming weeks, you will begin to see changes as we align our company Web sites. Please note that starting March 11, the login experience for our registered users will change as follows:

  • If you are a registered user of both djindexes.com and spindices.com:
    Starting March 11, your spindices.com user name and password will offer you registered-user access to both spindices.com and djindexes.com. Your current djindexes.com password will no longer be valid as of this date.

  • If you are a registered user of djindexes.com, but not spindices.com:
    Starting March 11, your djindexes.com user name will offer you registered-user access to both djindexes.com and spindices.com. As a registered user of spindices.com, you will be able to customize your preferences and save your own portfolio of indices, gaining instant access to indices that matter to you.

      • The first time you visit either djindexes.com or spindices.com after March 11, you will need to create a new password. This new password will be valid on both Web sites. Note that by creating this new password you will be agreeing to the new Terms of Use and Privacy Policy of both Web sites.

      If you have concerns about how these changes will impact you, please find our contact information online at http://www.djindexes.com/contactus.
