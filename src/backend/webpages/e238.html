    Jessie James Decker Wants How Many Children?! Watch the Eric & Jessie Star Reveal Her Big Family Goals

    by Mike Vulpo Sat., Sep. 6, 2014 2:59 PM PDT

    Loading...

    For Jessie James Decker, life with husband Eric Decker and their baby girl is pretty sweet.

    It's so great that the Eric & Jessie: Game On star wouldn't mind having a few more kids sooner rather than later.

    "I want like 20 more of them," the new mom told E! News exclusively at the Smirnoff Ice Ladies with Game tailgating event. "Eric and I were talking about that last night. He's like, ‘How many babies do you want?' and I'm like, ‘How many more you put in me, baby.'"

    All kidding aside, the country singer is loving every moment with her daughter, Vivianne Rose Decker.

    "I just never knew you could love someone so much, so fast," she gushed to E! News. "She is my heart and soul. She's on my mind 24/7. She's my everything." 

    PHOTOS: How cute is Eric and Jessie's baby girl?

    Courtesy Larry Busacca/Getty Images for City Of Hope

    In between mommy duties, Jessie has managed to squeeze in some time for workouts. In fact, the E! star was already back in her size-25 jeans less than five months after giving birth.

    "Oh my goodness! You know, just cardio and breastfeeding, little bit of both," she told E! News on how she got her body back. "It's pretty wild. I actually think [my body] is storing extra fat for the milk but I guess we'll see."

    With football season upon us, it's now going to be time for Jessie and Vivianne to root for dad on the New York Jets.

    Based on past social media posts, looks like Daddy's little girl is already a fan.

    "At daddy scrimmage!!," Jessie Instagrammed last month. "Vivi wanted her other headband apparently since the other fell off somewhere lol #jetsbaby" 

    —Reporting by James Chairman
