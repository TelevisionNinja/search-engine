    • GET TO KNOW THE HOSTS

    Adam Jones

    Before joining ESPNBoston.com in 2011, Jones had been working at 98.5 The Sports Hub hosting a show with Boston sports writer Dan Shaughnessy. Prior to The Hub, Jones worked at 890 ESPN from 2006-2009 covering all the Boston sports teams and doing SportsCenter updates.

    Drew Brooks

    Jones' sidekick and producer, Brooks hosted a morning show on the Sporting News affiliate The Fan 1060 in Phoenix from 2009-2010. Jones and Brooks worked together at 890 ESPN, where Brooks produced and did on-air updates.
