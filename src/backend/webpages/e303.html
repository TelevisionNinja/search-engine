    Kim Kardashian Gives Kanye West a Book of Selfies as a Wedding Gift—See the KUWTK Season Finale Recap!

    by Kamala Kirk Mon., Sep. 1, 2014 7:01 PM PDT

    Loading...

    On Sunday's season finale of Keeping Up With the Kardashians, we finally got to experience the amazing moment we've all been waiting for: Kim Kardashian marries Kanye West! But getting there isn't so easy...

    After a long flight, the family is finally in Paris! And with only five days to the wedding, there is still much to be done...

    Kris Jenner and the other members of the household meet up at Kanye's Paris apartment for the dress fitting.

    Unfortunately, none of the ladies are completely feeling their dresses and Kim can see that, but with such little time left before the wedding, there is little that can be done.

    Once Scott Disick shows up, the gang heads on over to see the Eiffel Tower!

    Scott can't help but feel slightly overwhelmed by everything:

    Kim and her family have to fight through the paparazzi as they make their way towards the Eiffel Tower!

    Later, Kim has the ladies in the family meet her at the Givenchy L'Atelier for her final dress fitting.

    Kim has to stop herself from talking when she almost ruins her big wedding surprise:

    Kim looks stunning as she models her gorgeous gown for everyone!

    The next day, Kim and all of her girlfriends meet up at Hotel Costes for an intimate dinner!

    Designer Lorraine Schwartz, Lala Anthony and others are in attendance, and everyone takes turns telling sweet stories about Kim and Kanye!

    After dinner, Kim spends the evening running around Paris with her girlfriends, seeing all of the beautiful sites and taking pictures!

    Bruce Jenner arrives for the wedding rehearsal but the bride is late...

    Finally Kim and the groom-to-be show up!

    Kim and Kanye try to make decisions with the wedding planner as they figure out the set-up and wedding procession, which turns out to be more complicated than it seems.

    Kim has another surprise for her sisters as well—Kanye was able to find another designer who made bridesmaid dresses for her sisters!

    After dealing with some style and fit issues, everyone agrees the second dress is perfect!

    The family starts the tedious task of getting every detail right during the wedding rehearsal.

    Bruce and Kanye share a sweet moment...

    Kim and Kanye even get silly when "practicing" their wedding day kiss:

    See the exciting rehearsal process!

    The next morning, the family boards a private plane to head over to Florence, Italy—which also happens to be where Kanye took Kim for her birthday back when they first got together.

    While on the plane, Kim remembers her crazy past few days. First, the family went to a private brunch at designer Valentino's estate!

    He gave everyone a tour of his gorgeous castle...

    And the entire family took an epic photo with him!

    But that wasn't the only surprise...

    They rented out the Palace of Versailles for the night to throw an extravagant party!

    But the next day, Khloé Kardashian pays for it due to her excessive partying the night before...

    They arrive in Florence at the stunning villa where the wedding will be held!

    With just two hours to the wedding, it's almost go time! Kim knows there is much to be done but she won't let anything get in her way:

    In all of the craziness, Kim puts the finishing touches on a brand new selfie book and reveals that she's giving it to Kanye as her wedding day gift!

    Awww...

    When Scott arrives he has some bad news: Rob Kardashian skipped the flight from Paris to Florence and ended up hopping on a flight straight back to Los Angeles.

    Kim is upset by the news but doesn't want to ruin her wedding so she decides to deal with it when she returns to Los Angeles.

    Oh, and did you see Scott's shoes?

    Classic Lord Disick.

    Then she has a mini freakout when she sees that Kylie Jenner's hair is still blue!

    But Kim realizes that it's too late now and instead of worrying about it, she needs to just relax, let go and be happy.

    Khloé's so hungover from the night before, she sleeps as her hair and makeup is done! 

    The wedding planner announces there are just five minutes left until Kim walks down the aisle...

    See the intense final moments!

    And it's finally time...

    There Kim goes!

    It's the best day of Kim's life and one that she'll never forget!
