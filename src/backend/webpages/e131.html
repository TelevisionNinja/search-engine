Kelly Clarkson Shares New Photo of Baby Daughter River Rose, Took Her to 1st Concert—See the Adorable Pic!

by Corinne Heller Sat., Sep. 6, 2014 8:39 AM PDT

Loading...
Twitter

Kelly Clarkson, you're killing us with the cuteness ...

The 32-year-old pop star brought her 2-month-old baby daughter River Rose to watch country star Garth Brooks and wife Trisha Yearwood perform near Chicago on Friday, the second show of his big comeback tour following a 13-year absence.

Clarkson shared on Twitter a photo of her adorable little munchkin, who is sucking on a blue pacifier and is wearing what appears to be a glittering pair of noise-cancelling headphones to protect her ears. River Rose is the singer's first baby and her first child with husband Brandon Blackstock.

"Got 2 sing with @TYcom tonight in Chicago & watched her and Garth as well! Amazing show! It was River's 1st concert!" Clarkson tweeted.

So people keep talking about the headphones that River is wearing in the last pic I posted. My girl Lesley made them! http://t.co/TF5IkzAEnz

- Kelly Clarkson (@kelly_clarkson) September 6, 2014

PHOTOS: Look back at Kelly's baby bump!

Clarkson, who shared the first public photo of her baby girl in June, had joined Yearwood on stage to perform their new duet, "Prizefighter." The latter star tweeted a photo of the two singing together, thanking Clarkson and calling her a "bad ass." 

Kelly @kelly_clarkson You are a bad ass! I love you!!! Thanks for coming to Chicago to sing with me! #PrizeFighter pic.twitter.com/nf2WScuNH1

? Trisha Yearwood (@TYcom) September 6, 2014

Fellow country star Carrie Underwood, who recently announced she is pregnant with her first child, also attended the show.

"Garth Brooks/Trisha Yearwood concert tonight in Chicago," she wrote on Instagram. "I may have gotten misty-eyed a few times. Hormones? Maybe. Amazing concert? Definitely."
