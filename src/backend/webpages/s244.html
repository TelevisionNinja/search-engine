Weather Delay

Photograph By Ezra Shaw/Getty Images

It's been 10 years since the Red Sox and Yankees engaged in one of the more memorable games in Boston's history. We're taking a look back at some of the images from the game, highlighted by the brawl between Jason Varitek and Alex Rodriguez and Bill Mueller's walk-off homer off Mariano Rivera. For all the excitement, the game almost didn't happen. After heavy rain the night before, the matchup was almost postponed. After a 54-minute delay, and some heavy campaigning from the Red Sox to play, the game got underway. "The Red Sox wanted to play today," Kevin Millar said. -- ESPN Boston (AP game recap »)
