    Miley Cyrus Goes Topless, Wears Nipple Pasties at NY Fashion Week After Party—See the Photos!

    by Corinne Heller Sun., Sep. 7, 2014 1:32 PM PDT

    Miley Cyrus was being as Miley as ever at New York Fashion Week this weekend.

    Cyrus, known for her risqué social media posts and recently appeared "busted ass naked," shared several photos and even a video of herself at the party on her Instagram page.
