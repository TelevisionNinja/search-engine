Real-Time and Delayed Index Data

S&P Dow Jones Indices licenses the real-time and delayed index values of Dow Jones-branded indices—including the Dow Jones Industrial Average as well as thousands of other indices—many of which are licensed as the basis of ETFs, mutual funds and other index-linked investment products. The complete list of Dow Jones indices included in the license agreement is located in the first and second tabs of the Excel file available on our Symbols & Calendars page.

S&P DJI calculates the Dow Jones indices, which are then disseminated via the distribution gateway, CME Group, and distributed to our direct and indirect vendors.

In order to be an authorized distributor of Dow Jones indices, a license agreement must be executed with the applicable fee. The agreement is tailored to a company's specific usage and may include index display and distribution on media and platforms including, but not limited to the following:

  • Internet sites
  • Terminal products
  • Handheld devices
  • Wallboards
  • Tickers
  • Extranet or intranet services
  • Television programs
  • Other electronic products and services

As a service to our clients, those who license intraday Dow Jones indices receive weekly updates announcing new index launches, index corrections, outages, and other information. Our clients can be sure that they are in the know first.

All requests and inquiries for the intraday Dow Jones indices should be sent to RTData_Indexes@spdji.com.

Any unauthorized display, re-distribution or other commercial use of the real-time and delayed indices constitutes misappropriation of S&P Dow Jones Indices' proprietary rights in and to its stock indexes and infringement of the related trademarks.
