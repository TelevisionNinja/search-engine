Historical Timeline

2012
Jul. 2 The McGraw-Hill Companies and CME Group announce the launch of S&P Dow Jones Indices.
Apr. 30 Dow Jones Indexes named Most Innovative ETF Index Provider for the Americas.

2011
Oct. 18 The Europe Dow and The Asia Dow are introduced as regional counterparts to the Dow Jones Industrial Average.
Sep. 13 The Dow Jones Volatility Risk Control Indexes are launched.
May 4 Dow Jones Indexes and FXCM jointly launch the Dow Jones FXCM Dollar Index.
Apr. 25 The Dow Jones Islamic Market Indexes receive their 25th industry award.

2010
Nov. 17 Dow Jones Indexes introduces the Dow Jones Golden Crossover Indexes.
Oct. 28 Dow Jones Indexes and Sand Hill Econometrics announce the creation of the Dow Jones U.S. Venture Capital Index.
Jul. 15 Dow Jones Indexes announces the launch of the Dow Jones Long-Term Inflation Indexes.
Jun. 22 Credit Suisse and Dow Jones Indexes signed an agreement which covers the calculation, licensing, branding and marketing of the hedge fund indexes formerly known as the Credit Suisse/Tremont Hedge Fund Indexes.
Mar. 18 CME Group Inc. and Dow Jones & Company announce the launch of the new joint venture company CME Group Index Services LLC.
Jan. 27 Dow Jones Indexes named "Index Provider of the Year in Asia" by Asia Asset Management magazine.

2009
May 27 Dow Jones Indexes announces the launch of the Dow Jones U.S. Economic Stimulus Index.
May 12 Dow Jones Indexes is named "Most Innovative ETF Index Provider—Americas" for the second consecutive year.
May 7 The widely adopted Dow Jones-AIG Commodity Indexes are rebranded as the Dow Jones-UBS Commodity Indexes.
Apr. 1 Dow Jones Total Stock Market Indexes, the successor to the Dow Jones Wilshire Index family, are introduced.
Jan. 22 Dow Jones Indexes is named "Best Islamic Index Provider" in the Islamic Finance News Poll 2008.

2008
Nov. 11 The Global Dow is launched.
Jul. 14 Dow Jones Indexes and Brookfield Asset Management launch a series of global infrastructure indexes.
Jun. 4 Dow Jones Indexes is named the "Most Innovative ETF Index Provider of 2007" for the Americas at the 4th annual Global ETF Awards hosted by Exchangetradedfunds.com.
Apr. 14 Dow Jones Indexes opens an office in Dubai, its first in the Middle East.
Apr. 7 PIMCO announces the launch of five funds based on the recently-launched Dow Jones Real Return Target Date Indexes.
Mar. 11 Dow Jones Indexes and Transparent Value LLC launch 130/30 indexes based on the RBP® analytic.
Jan. 17 Dow Jones Indexes is named the "Best Islamic Index Provider" by Islamic Finance News.

2007
Dec. 11 Dow Jones Indexes wins the "Best Islamic Index Provider" category of the inaugural Master of Islamic Finance Awards by Terrapin Ltd.
Sep. 4 Dow Jones Indexes and Barron's announce the launch of the Barron's 400 Index.
Mar. 29 Dow Jones Indexes and STOXX Ltd. are named "Number One Index provider of the Year 2007 Germany" by the U.K. based magazine Structured Products. This ranking is based on a survey of more than 100 structured product providers.
Feb. 1 Eleven Dow Jones U.S. Sector Indexes are licensed to ProShares Trust and its affiliates to serve as the basis of 22 leveraged and inverse ETFs.

2006
Nov. 28 Dow Jones Indexes is named "Index Provider of the Year, Asia" by the U.K. based magazine Structured Products based on the votes of an independent judging panel.
May 16 Dow Jones Indexes commemorates the 110th anniversary of the Dow Jones Industrial Average, created by Charles Dow on May 26, 1896.
Jan. 23 Dow Jones Indexes and SAM Group, a pioneer in sustainability, launch the Dow Jones Islamic Market Sustainability Index.

2005
Mar. 31 Dow Jones Indexes launches first "target date" index series.

2004
Sep. 6 Dow Jones Indexes and China Business Network Co. Ltd. (CBN) launch the Dow Jones CBN China 600 Index.
Sep. 2 Dow Jones Indexes licenses the Dow Jones Italy Titans 30 Index to Indexchange to be used as the underlying for an ETF.
Jun. 14 Dow Jones Indexes licenses the Dow Jones China 88 Index to Yinhua Fund Management Co., Ltd., to be used as the underlying for an enhanced index fund.
Apr. 21 The Chicago Board of Trade (CBOT®) and Dow Jones Indexes announce that they will launch the Dow Jones CBOT Treasury Index, the first real-time benchmark to track the U.S. Treasury market.
Feb. 19 Dow Jones Indexes and FTSE Group announce that they will jointly market a sector classification system.

2003
Dec. 29 Singapore Exchange Limited (SGX) and Dow Jones Indexes sign a memorandum of understand to pioneer the development of exchange traded credit derivative contracts.
Nov. 3 The Dow Jones (U.S.) Select Dividend Index is launched.
Oct. 28 Dow Jones Indexes announces the Dow Jones Hedge Fund Strategy Benchmarks.
Oct. 6 Dow Jones Indexes and Dubai International Financial Centre launch the first Middle East blue-chip index for the Arabia Region.
Jun. 18 The first Mexican mutual funds based on the Dow Jones Industrial Average are launched by Actinver Fondos de Inversión and Santander Serfín.
Feb. 18 Dow Jones Islamic Market Blue-Chip Indexes are launched.

2002
Sep. 18 Mini-Dow futures contracts ($5) launch on the CBOT.
May 13 CBOE launches options on DIAMONDS.
Apr. 12 Dow Jones Country Titans Indexes are launched for seven European markets.

2001
Sep. 24 Dow Jones Industrial Average begins "Indicative" calculation at 3 A.M. EDT.
Feb. 15 The Dow Jones Sector Titans Indexes are launched.

2000
Sep. 18 The Dow Jones Global Indexes moves to free-float weighting and 95% developed-market coverage.
Jun. 26 The Dow Jones U.S. Style Indexes are launched.
May 26 BGI introduces the first ETFs based on the new Dow Jones market sector indexes.
Feb. 14 The Dow Jones U.S. Total Market Index is launched; the Dow Jones Global Industry Classification Structure is introduced.

1999
Nov. 15 The Market's Measure becomes available in bookstores.
Oct. 18 Assets in DIAMONDS trust reach $1 Billion.
Sep. 7 The Dow Jones Sustainability Group Indexes are launched.
Jul. 14 The Dow Jones Global Titans Index is launched.
Apr. 29 American Equity Life introduces the first fixed annuity based on the Dow Jones Industrial Average.
Apr. 1 The inaugural issue of INDEXES: The Journal of Index Issues (now the Journal of Indexes) is published.
Mar. 30 The Dow Jones Industrial Average closes above 10000 for the first time.
Feb. 9 The Dow Jones Islamic Market Index is launched.
Jan. 5 The Dow Jones-AIG Commodity Index is launched.
Jan. 4 The Dow 10 Cash Option begins trading on the CBOE.

1998
Mar. 16 Waterhouse launches the first fund based on the Dow Jones Industrial Average.
Feb. 26 The Dow Jones STOXX Indexes are launched.
Jan. 20 DIAMONDS begin trading on the NYSE Alternext, with record breaking volume for the first day of trading—tops 1,700,000 units.

1997
Nov. 6 CBOE's options on the Dow Jones Industrial Average jump to the third most active index options product in the U.S.
Oct. 6 Cash options and cash futures begin trading on the Dow Jones Industrial Average on the CBOE and CBOT, respectively.
Jun. 6 The Dow is first licensed as basis of investment products.
