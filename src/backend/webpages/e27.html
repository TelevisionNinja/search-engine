    Nikki Bella Keeps a Big Secret From John Cena and Rosa Mendes Reveals Rehab Stint—See the Total Divas Recap!

    by Kamala Kirk Sun., Sep. 7, 2014 7:01 PM PDT

    Loading...

    Fresh off the heels of WrestleMania and her big wedding to WWE Superstar Daniel Bryan, Brie Bella and the other Divas are back for another season of excitement and drama!

    Check out the cute picture above of the power couple celebrating Daniel's big win! Go Braniel!

    To make up for eloping and not telling her parents, Eva Marie and husband Jonathan Coyle have decided to have a wedding ceremony so that their families can celebrate with them.

    Prior to wedding planning, the couple gets engagement photos taken. But these ones are extra risqué...what will her family think?

    It's Monday Night Raw and we meet the newest Diva, Rosa Mendes!

    Rosa isn't new to the wrestling world, however. She has been in the WWE for 8 years and is returning after being on hiatus for a year and a half.

    She's more determined than ever for a shot at the Divas Championship!

    Nattie immediately notices that Rosa's boobs are hanging out of her top and warns her that she needs to maintain a classy appearance, especially since it's her first day back.

    Nattie thinks that Rosa looks like she's in a Frederick's of Hollywood photo shoot:

    At lunch, Eva Marie shows her engagement photos to Trinity and Ariane to get input. They're both surprised by how racy the pictures are.

    WWE Superstar Titus O'Neal gives his opinion:

    The next day at lunch, Brie asks Nikki Bella how things are going with boyfriend John Cena. Nikki says they're okay but mentions how John asked her a strange question:

    Nikki feels stuck because she thinks John is the One, but she also doesn't want to sacrifice her dream of being a mother.

    Then Brie comes up with an interesting idea:

    Brie thinks it would be a good way for Nikki to protect herself and to ensure that she can still have kids down the road, just in case things don't work out with John.

    Watch the interesting exchange and see if Nikki thinks it's a good idea!

     

    In a meeting right before Friday Night Smackdown, Rosa is surprised by the cold reception that the other Divas give her:

    Brie has her own reasons, however:

    When Mark Carrano, the Senior Director of Talent Relations, asks if anyone has talked to Summer Rae, his question is met with an awkward silence.

    Because she is off filming a movie in Vancouver, none of the Divas have seen or spoken to her. That should mean less drama, right?

    Mark pulls Nattie aside after the meeting because he wants her to help out Rosa and share a room with her when they travel. Nattie isn't exactly ecstatic when she hears the news.

    Nattie is concerned that hanging out with Rosa will taint her reputation but she promises Mark that she'll do it.

    See the intense exchange!

    Later, Nattie tells Rosa that she was assigned to take care of her, which makes Rosa feel like no one trusts her.

    Then she reveals the truth about her absence:

    Rosa explains that she had gone through a lot of stress and a tough relationship but she's grateful for the second chance that WWE gave her and she wants to prove that she can do it.

    Brie and Nikki go to lunch with their brother JJ, his wife Lola, and Brie's husband Daniel when the topic of babies comes up.

    Nikki surprises her brother by informing him that she has decided to freeze her eggs even though she would probably choose John over kids, but Brie is happy with her sister's decision to support her future.

    Eva Marie and Jonathan visit her parents to announce their wedding plans. Her father is elated that he finally gets to walk his daughter down the aisle!

    But her family's reaction to her engagement photos isn't so great:

    Brie accompanies Nikki to the fertility clinic where they meet with a doctor about freezing her eggs.

    In addition to getting bloodwork to check her hormone levels, Nikki finds out that she can't drink or have sex during the process.

    During lunch with Eva Marie's family, everyone starts discussing the wedding plans with excitement.

    But her brothers are shocked when they find out about her plans for the ceremony.

    Jonathan explains that he isn't a Catholic but they don't seem to understand.

    Jonathan refuses to change his religious views to have a traditional wedding, which heavily disappoints Eva Marie's father.

    It looks like this isn't going to be as easy as she thought...

    At John and Nikki's house, they meet with their interior designer for the final touches on their home. But halfway through the appointment, Nikki remembers that she scheduled for a nurse to come to the house to draw her blood.

    Oh, shoot...

    Nikki decides to distract John by sending him and the interior designer into another room so that she can hurry to the other end of the house and have her blood drawn without him knowing.

    The nurse hurries in and Nikki gets her blood drawn quickly before John finds out. She lets out a sigh of relief!

    Nattie is surprised by a naked Rosa when she shows up to their hotel room!

    The whole experience makes her very uncomfortable...

    But Rosa is proud of her "assets"...

    While running errands with her mom, Eva Marie finds out that her dad's cancer has returned and has gotten even worse. She is devastated by the news!

    Back at home, Eva Marie's brother explains that their dad's condition is inoperable and that all they can do is treat the cancer and try to slow it down.

    Back at the clinic, Nikki gets an ultrasound to check on her ovaries. But when the doctor positions her on the table, Brie gets a little more than she bargained for...

    The doctor points out the lining of Nikki's uterus and says that she has plenty of eggs, which is a good sign!

    After, the nurse tells her about a 10-day medication that Nikki needs to administer to herself as the next step in the process. The only problem is that she needs to inject it!

    And will she be able to do it without John finding out?

    After going for a run, Eva Marie asks Jonathan if he would consider converting to make her dad happy.

    Ever since she found out about his cancer returning, Eva Marie has been feeling extra guilty and wants to make things easier for her dad.

    Jonathan explains that he can't go against his own personal and spiritual beliefs to make someone else happy and refuses to compromise on this one thing.

    But Eva Marie is still determined to make things right for her father:

    It's the night of Rosa's first match and she's super nervous. It's been over a year since she stepped foot into the ring!

    But when she overhears some of the other Divas talking negatively about her, Rosa becomes visibly upset.

    However, she wipes her tears away and heads into the ring to face off against Nattie!

    Natiie ends up winning the match and Rosa is not happy with the results:

    Nattie gives Rosa a pep talk and tells her to keep her head high, ignore the haters and keep believing in herself.

    Rosa becomes remotivated and can't wait for her next rematch!

    Back at home, John catches Nikki off-guard when she sees him holding her medication.

    Oh snap!

    What will happen when Nikki tells the truth to John? Or will she be able to lie her way out of this one and still keep her plan a secret?

    Tune-in for an all-new episode of Total Divas next Sunday at 9/8c on E!
