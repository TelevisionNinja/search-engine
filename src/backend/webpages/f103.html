Do I need a license to launch a financial product based on your indices?

Yes, a license is required to launch any product whose performance is linked to the value of a particular index and for all use of company name or index names in the marketing of the product. License fees differ according to the type of product. For further information please visit our Real Time and Delayed Index Data page.
